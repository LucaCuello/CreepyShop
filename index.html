About us

Our story

This brand is a result from the love that we all have for spooky things. We always wanted to do something about it but couldn’t figure out what, until someone said "hey, what about a shop with collectible things, such as mugs and clothing". That was it, we started planning this amazing dream of ours then and we never stopped. Today we are proud to say we have incredible products and carefully selected items that other horror lovers can take home too! We couldn’t have done it without the support from all of you, so from the bottom of our hearts, thank you!
